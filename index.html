a one day conference

The annual conference for digital creatives.

One Day Out is an international single day conference held in the heart of Denmark, Odense. The goal of the conference is to gather people for a day of inspiration and meeting new people.

The conference is joined by people from all over Europe to watch and listen to creative and smart people. Our speakers come to Odense, from all over the world, to tell about their story, to share their experiences or show some of their greatest work.

The conference is a non-profit initiative by the DEVDK.org community. DEVDK is a Danish online community with a Slack channel of 600+ users. All profit we get will be put into next years conference and other community initiatives. We hope everyone joining One Day Out will go home inspired, happy and with new friendships.

Our Manifesto

A conference for everyone

We welcome everyone to join us to take one day out of their calendars, meet new people and listen to interesting talks by people we admire.

It’s all about the community

We have always wanted the conference to be for the community by the community. This is why we listen to the voices of our audience and try to learn from it. We moved from being a developer focus conference to a design and front-end development focused by listing to our community.

We truly admire our speakers

Our speakers are makers, designers, entrepreneurs and everything in between. We try to find speakers that are passionated and can inspire people. We don’t dictate any topics on our speakers, but invite them to pick a topic they are truly passionated about.
